# Ship Classes

## Vocabulary

- Class: a role category, such as Miner, Scout, Fighter, or Support.
- Hull: a concrete ship model inside a class.
- Ship instance: a persistent account-owned copy of a hull.

Do not define final numeric stats in this document.

## Miner

Purpose:

- resource extraction;
- cargo capacity;
- safe tutorial mining;
- pilot automation introduction.

Tradeoffs:

- weak combat;
- low speed;
- vulnerable outside protected zones.

## Scout

Purpose:

- first combat ship;
- exploration;
- interception;
- learning frontline movement and weapons.

Tradeoffs:

- high speed;
- high maneuverability;
- low HP;
- weaker sustained damage than heavier classes.

## Fighter

Purpose:

- basic main combat class;
- stronger frontline participation;
- better durability and weapons than Scout.

Tradeoffs:

- larger;
- slower than Scout;
- less focused on mining and utility.

## Support

Working-name class. The role is not approved yet.

Possible directions:

- repair;
- shields;
- electronic warfare;
- logistics;
- command buffs.

## Ownership Flexibility

A player may eventually own multiple hulls of the same class if later design allows it. Class unlocks and ship ownership are separate decisions.
