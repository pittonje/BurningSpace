# Squads And Squadrons

## Terms

Hangar:

The complete account-owned collection of ship instances.

Active ship:

The ship currently controlled personally by the human player.

Wing:

A small active group owned by one player.

Squadron:

A later expanded player group, larger than a wing.

## Recommended Starting Model

- one human-controlled ship;
- zero to two pilot-controlled wingmen;
- later account progression can increase the limit;
- the server defines the maximum active size;
- not every hangar ship can be deployed at once.

## Pilot Commands

Possible command set:

- follow;
- keep formation;
- attack my target;
- defend me;
- defend area;
- mine selected zone;
- return to base;
- retreat at health threshold;
- maintain distance;
- do not engage players;
- engage NPC only;
- engage all enemies.

## Design Boundary

Complex visual behavior editing is a separate future system. Initial behavior should be server-defined presets with clear acceptance rules.
