# ADR 0002: Horizontal Account Progression

## Status

Proposed

## Context

The new concept adds accounts, hangars, classes, pilots, and long-term progression. Strong vertical power risks making new players irrelevant.

## Decision

Progression should primarily unlock roles, configurations, pilot behaviors, cosmetics, and strategic options rather than large raw combat advantages.

## Consequences

- More design work is needed for tradeoffs.
- Monetization and rewards must avoid pay-to-win.
- Veterans gain breadth and expression more than guaranteed victory.

## Alternatives

- Vertical RPG-style power growth.
- Purely cosmetic progression.
- Seasonal resets with strong temporary power growth.

## Open Questions

- What progression rewards are acceptable?
- Should account level affect deployment limits?
- How much catch-up help should new players receive?
